Chicago Art Gallery

Explore thousands of artworks in the museum’s collection—from our renowned icons to lesser-known works from every corner of the globe—as well as our books, writings, reference materials, and other resources.

The Art Institute of Chicago is a museum and school for the fine arts. It was founded in 1879 and has a permanent collection of nearly 300,000 works of art. The collection includes Chinese bronzes, contemporary design, textiles, and installation art. The Art Institute of Chicago is the second largest art museum in the United States. It's also the only museum in the world to be ranked among the world's best by TripAdvisor four years in a row.

The Art Institute of Chicago encourages visitors to take pictures of the collection and special exhibitions for personal use. Non-flash photography is permitted in all permanent galleries.
