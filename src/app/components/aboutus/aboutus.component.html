How To Use the Application

  1. In home page, you can see the introduction of chicago art gallery.
  2. click on artworks on navbar or click on "click me to view art" button in homepage to see all artworks available in chicago art gallery
  3. each card contains art image, art name, artist name and date date_display. click on ReadMore to read description exhibition_history and publication_history of artwork.
  4. use search box to search through the artwork
  5. click on like button to mark the art as favourites
  6. click on Wishlist in navbar to see your favourites
